Our Laboratory

What is Absurdo Lab?

Were a media lab, a hackspace, an absurd laboratory or maybe we dont fit in any.This is a place to experiment, to create, a space for digital thinking. We want to re-imagine and re-think the future through technology, we observe and transform digital.

In here, we are interested in global advances to change local realities, we like the freedom to experiment, the knowledge transfer and the collective creation as a tool for testing technology from other perspectives. We create meaningful design, social design.
